Wellness Rituals

Three mindful ways To Enjoy Himalayan Honey

Immunity Dawn Tonic

Blend Van Amrit wild honey with warm water, ginger, and tulsi leaves to energise mornings and support breathing.

  • • 1 tsp honey stirred into 150 ml warm water
  • • Add crushed ginger + 3 tulsi leaves
  • • Sip mindfully before breakfast

Radiance Skin Mask

Use Him Kanti white honey with saffron and sandalwood to calm and brighten skin during weekly at-home rituals.

  • • 1 tsp honey + 1 pinch saffron
  • • Add sandalwood for a soothing paste
  • • Apply 10 minutes before rinse

Moonlight Wind-Down

Stir Kashtha Bal chestnut honey into chamomile tea with a dash of nutmeg to relax muscles and mind.

  • • Brew caffeine-free chamomile
  • • Sweeten with 1 tsp honey
  • • Sprinkle nutmeg & breathe deep
