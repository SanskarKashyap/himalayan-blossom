Cancellation & Refund Policy

Your Satisfaction is Our Priority

1. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it. To cancel, please contact us at hello@himalayanblossom.com or call us at +91 99308 15228. If the order has already been shipped, it cannot be canceled.

2. Refunds

We are dedicated to providing the highest quality honey products. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase:

  • Damaged or Defective Items: If you receive a damaged jar or if the product is defective, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We will send a replacement or issue a full refund.
  • Incorrect Items: If we shipped the wrong product, we will exchange it free of charge.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Due to the nature of our products (food items), we generally do not accept returns for reasons of taste preferences. However, please contact us if you have concerns, and we will do our best to find a satisfactory solution.

4. Processing Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days back to your original payment method.

5. Quality Guarantee

We stand behind the purity and quality of our Himalayan honey. Every jar is traceable and lab-tested.
