About Us

Meet Deen Dayal & The Hive

Founder

Deen Dayal — the "Bee Father of India"

Deen Dayal has spent over 20 years hand-tending Himalayan hives. Blending ancestral beekeeping with modern AI hive monitoring, he harvests honey that is pure, raw, and traceable to a single valley. Our mission is to empower local farmers with sustainable livelihoods, bring beekeeping education to schools, and deliver an ethical luxury honey to homes worldwide.

Why Choose Himalayan Blossom

Pure, small-batch honey crafted with care — traceable to valley and harvest, ethically harvested, and presented in luxury packaging for a thoughtful unboxing.
